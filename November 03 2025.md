$$
\int \frac{1}{x^{2}+2x+17} \, dx = \int \frac{1}{(x+1)^{2}+16} \, dx
$$
$$
= \int \frac{1}{4^{2}+(x-1)^{2}} \, dx = \frac{1}{4^{2}}\int \frac{1}{1+\left( \frac{x+1}{4} \right)^{2}}
$$
$$
u = \frac{x+1}{4}
$$
$$
4du = dx 
$$
$$
= \frac{4}{4^{2}}\int \frac{1}{1 + u^{2}} \, du = \frac{4}{4^{2}}\arctan(u) + C 
$$
$$
= \frac{1}{4}\arctan\left( \frac{x+1}{4} \right) + C
$$

## The area of an Ellipse.

$$
\frac{x^{2}}{a^{2}} + \frac{y^{2}}{b^{2}} = 1
$$
$$
\frac{y^{2}}{b^{2}} = 1 - \frac{x^{2}}{a^{2}}
$$
$$
y = \pm \sqrt{ b^{2}\left( 1-\frac{x^{2}}{a^{2}} \right) }
$$
$$
y = b\sqrt{ 1-\frac{x^{2}}{a^{2}} }
$$

Integrate this.
